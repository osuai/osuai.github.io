Welcome to OSU AI Club!

The Artificial Intelligence Club is a student run organization that meets regularly to discuss topics and work on projects in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. OSU Artificial Intelligence Club mainly consists of students in the EECS program; however, all are welcome to join who have a passion for learning about Artificial Intelligence.

What We Do

The members of the club believe that Artificial Intelligence is a large player in science and will shape society as a whole. The members would like to encourage those interested in exploring Artificial Intelligence to check out the club and see if Artificial Intelligence fits their interests. The club is actively seeking students who would like to learn or develop projects. The club aims to build applications that demonstrate the capabilities of current Artificial Intelligence technologies. Eventually the club hopes to use these applications in real world settings and in fun demonstrations.
